About Me

Originally from New Jersey, I moved to Massachusetts to continue my education. I am currently a junior at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) working towards my BS in Computer Science and a minor in Data Science. I will graduate in May 2023. I have loved technology for as long as I can remember and am always thinking about how I can create applications that will benefit myself and others. My career goal is to make technology accessible to everyone by designing intuitive webpages that are easy to navigate and simple to master.

When I am not sitting behind my laptop, you can find me tinkering and upgrading my 3D printer, tearing up the slopes (weather dependent), or climbing some trees. I love a good adventure and even spent two months in Prague studying abroad. At WPI I am fairly invloved: I am a campus tour guide, a Web Developer for the Marketing Department, and an active member of Phi Sigma Sigma Women's Fraternity and the Student Alumni Society (SAS).

Universitea

Two true facts:
1. There are thousands of colleges in the United States.
2. I was (and still am) a very indecisive person.
This led to the creation of Universitea. Your one-stop shop to finding the college that is your perfect cup of tea. Many college search engines can filter by which school is the best for Computer Science or which has the best food on campus, but I wanted to know everything at once, and Universitea was born! Universitea gives users a comprehensive list of everything they're looking for, and even filters for safety, target, and reach schools. Visit Universitea here.
